Cookie policy

Fairyland Cottage keeps tracking to a minimum. The site does not use Google Analytics, advertising pixels, or a cookie banner for non-essential marketing cookies.

Services used

  • The contact form uses EmailJS to send messages. EmailJS receives the details you submit in the form so the message can be delivered.
  • The shop uses a PayPal hosted payment button. PayPal may use cookies or similar technologies when you open PayPal checkout.
  • The site loads fonts, icons, and payment images from third-party services needed to display the pages correctly.

Your choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. If you block third-party services, some features such as the contact form or PayPal checkout may not work as expected.

If you have a question about privacy or cookies, please use the contact page.
